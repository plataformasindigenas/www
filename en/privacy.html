Privacy Policy

How we handle your personal data in compliance with LGPD.

Data Collected

This website collects personal data exclusively through the contact form, processed by formsubmit.co. The data collected includes:

  • Name
  • Email
  • Organization
  • Message

Purpose of Use

The collected data is used exclusively to respond to inquiries and contact requests. We do not share, sell, or use your data for any other purpose.

Cookies and Tracking

This is a static website and does not use cookies, trackers, or third-party analytics. No browsing data is collected or stored.

External Services

This website loads fonts from Google Fonts servers. When accessing the site, your browser may send data to Google as per Google's privacy policy.

Your Rights (LGPD)

In compliance with Brazil's General Data Protection Law (LGPD - Law No. 13,709/2018), you have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction of incomplete or outdated data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Revoke consent for the use of your data

Data Deletion

To request the deletion of your personal data, please contact us at info@plataformasindigenas.org.
